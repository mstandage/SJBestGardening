About us

At SJ Best Gardening, we’re passionate about helping people enjoy their gardens all year round. Based in Oxfordshire, we serve local communities in Abingdon, Oxford, Kennington, Wallingford, Wantage, and surrounding areas.

With a friendly and professional approach, and over two decades of experience, we believe every outdoor space has potential. Whether it’s a small town garden or a large countryside plot, we bring the same care and attention to detail—because your garden deserves to look its best.

Our motto, “Making your bit of nature beautiful”, reflects our commitment to turning everyday gardens into relaxing, enjoyable spaces.

Services

We provide a full range of gardening services, including:

  • Lawn Care and Mowing – keeping your grass neat, healthy, and vibrant
  • Hedge Trimming and Pruning – shaping and maintaining hedges and shrubs
  • Garden Tidy-Ups – seasonal clearances and one-off transformations
  • Planting and Borders – adding colour, structure, and life to your garden
  • Weeding and Maintenance – keeping flowerbeds and paths under control
  • Leaf Clearance and Waste Removal – ensuring your space stays clean and usable

Whether you’re after a one-off visit or regular garden maintenance, we’re here to take the hard work off your hands.
